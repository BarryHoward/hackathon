About Us
  • Barry Howard, Front-End Developer

  • While studying coding at the Iron Yard in Atlanta, developers Barry Howard and Snehel Patel discovered they shared a passion for photography and other forms of visual media, and thus Instashare was born!

    In Instagram, users have a platform to show off their photos while organizing them based on on a simple like sytem. Unlike other sharing services, there are no user profiles or histories, the goal being to encourage a more dynamic experience and discourage any lasting user conflicts. This frees the application up for more organic discussion that supports Barry and Snehel's unorthodox style.
